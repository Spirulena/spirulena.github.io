David

Our founder, David, possesses over a decade of diverse experience in consulting, startup leadership, and mentorship, driven by an unyielding passion for technology and open source. Whether delving into new programming languages or guiding the next generation of thought leaders, his dedication remains unwavering.David has held various roles, including Data Engineer, Data Scientist, Lead Programmer, Head of Data, DevOps Platform Engineer, CTO, and Founder of small startups, reflecting his commitment to drawing wisdom from diverse sources to navigate the ever-evolving tech landscape. Notably, he has applied his expertise across a spectrum of industries, showcasing adaptability and a keen ability to contribute in different domains.An astrophysicist by trade, David embarked on a unique career trajectory, making significant contributions to renowned companies like Square Enix and Activision Blizzard. Despite his academic roots in astrophysics, he seamlessly transitioned into innovative gaming and entertainment environments, exemplifying his versatility across industries.
